About

The focus of the lab is to develop high-performance applications to deal with real-world problems in bioinformatics and medicine. We strongly work to use academic research to fulfill the demands of the industry. Currently, our main interest is in Computational Pathology.

Having sponsors like UEFS (Universidade Estadual de Feira de Santana) and Fapesb, we strive to bridge the gap between academic research and practical applications in the field of high-performance computing.

Our lab is located at Universidade Estadual de Feira de Santana, Department of Technology / LABOTEC 3, Av. Transnordestina, s/n – Novo Horizonte, 44036-900, Feira de Santana, Bahia, Brazil.
